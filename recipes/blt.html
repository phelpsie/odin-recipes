The Definitive BLT Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 slices of bread (toasted)
  • 2-4 slices of bacon
  • Tomato
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 1 tbsp of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cook 2-4 slices of bacon in a skillet until cooked to your desired crispiness. Remove and let dry on a paper towel placed on a plate.
  2. Slice two slices of tomato
  3. Wash two leaves of lettuce under running water
  4. Spread 1 tbsp of mayonnaise on 1 toasted slice of bread
  5. Arrange bacon, lettuce, and tomato on top, and close the sandwich with a the other slice of toasted bread

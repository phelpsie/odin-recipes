The Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 slices of bread (untoasted)
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Condiment of choice is optional
  • Butter or Non-Stick substance if necessary for your pan or panini grill

Steps

  1. Shred or slice desired amount of cheddar cheese for sandwich
  2. Place cheddar cheese on one slice of sandwich and close with the other slice
  3. *If using a microwave, place inside, and heat until cheese is thoroughly melted*
  4. *If using a pan or panini grill that requires it, ensure surface is buttered or coated with non-stick material before grilling
  5. *Optional* Dip grilled cheese in condiment of choice

Tuna Melt Recipe

A Tuna Melt served english muffin style

This one is sure to delight, and switches it up with an open face style.

Ingrediants

  • Tuna (preferably Light Tuna)
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Stalk of Celery
  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 1 Cheddar Cheese block

Steps

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Drain tuna water from can
  3. Mix tuna with mayonnaise to your desired mixture
  4. Chop carrots and celery and add to the bowl of tuna
  5. Spread on toasted slice of bread to a medium to medium-heavy thickness
  6. Slice a medium thickness slice of cheddar cheese and place on top of tuna
  7. *Optional* If cheese does not adequately cover (less than one half to two thirds) the open faced sandwich, add another slice or partial slice to your liking
